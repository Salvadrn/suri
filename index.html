Education is a right, not a privilege

Over 430 million people worldwide live with disabling hearing loss — yet the vast majority of educational video content is published without any form of caption or accessibility support.

Sound2Text bridges that gap. By combining advanced AI with thoughtful design, we make it possible for educators, institutions, and content creators to ensure that every learner — regardless of hearing ability — can access knowledge equally.
